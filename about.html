The Social Security System

The Social Security System (SSS) is a government agency in the Philippines that provides social security protection to workers in the private, professional, and informal sectors.

Established under Republic Act No. 1161, as amended by RA 8282 (Social Security Act of 1997), the SSS ensures that its members and their families are protected against hazards such as sickness, maternity, disability, old age, death, and other contingencies resulting in loss of income or financial burden.

The SSS is committed to delivering prompt, efficient, and reliable social security services to all its members across the nation.
